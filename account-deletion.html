Account Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your Topik Champ account, please follow the steps below. Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Go to Home and ‚Å†tap on menu icon (on the Left - Top corner of the Home, three horizontal lines)
  2. Click Delete Account
  3. Check the check-box if you agree
  4. Click Delete My Account button

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal information will be permanently removed from our systems
  • Your username will become available for others to use
  • Your posts and comments will be anonymized but remain on the platform
  • You will lose access to all content you've created
  • Any active subscriptions will be canceled

Please note that some information may be retained for legal, security, or business purposes as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
